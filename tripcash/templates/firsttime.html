With Tripcash, you can easily manage all of your travel expenses with just a few clicks. Organize your expenses by labels, both big and small, and keep track of your spending to help plan your budget more effectively.

By default, Tripcash comes with some generic labels, but you can easily edit and delete them, or even create your own custom labels that better suit your travel needs.

To get started with Tripcash, simply create your first trip by giving it a name. Remember to choose a unique name that hasn't been used for any other trips before.
